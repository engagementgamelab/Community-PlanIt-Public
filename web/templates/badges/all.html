Mover & Shaker
You can earn the Mover and Shaker badge by being one of the top 25 point-earners at the half point mark. If you maintain your standing through to the end, you’ll be a level 2 Mover and Shaker.
Visionary
You can earn the Visionary badge by completing every challenge available in a mission. While it’s important to increase your presence on the map, it’s just as important to contribute all you can to the game. The more you share and engage, the more your community benefits. Level up your Visionary status each time you complete every challenge in a mission.
Local Sage
By answering ten trivia challenges correctly, you can earn the Local Sage badge. It’s a real demonstration of local knowledge. If you answer another ten correctly, you can earn level two Sage status!
Crowdsourcer
Bringing people together is the name of the game, and the crowdsourcer badge goes to anyone who creates a get together with more than ten attendees. You can earn another level of Crowdsourcer by using your organizing skills to bring together two more crowds with ten or more players.
Hometown Hero
Can’t get any better than this. You can earn the Hometown Hero badge by finishing the game as the player with the most points. Only one person per game can win this badge. It comes with lots of bragging rights.
