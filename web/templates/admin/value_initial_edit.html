Values

Within each instance of Community PlanIt, players earn PlanIt Tokens, which they use to vote (or “spend”) on values important to them. The values in your instance of Community PlanIt should relate directly to aspects of your planning process; think about the broad categories that the planning process impacts.


In our Chinatown Master Plan example, these values included: Open Space, Housing Choice, Shopping and Dining, Local Businesses, Parking, and Identity.

For your game, you should provide between 5 and 7 succinct values for users to consider when they spend their coins.
