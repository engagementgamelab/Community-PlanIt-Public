  • Community PlanIt is an engagement game that gives you the power to plan your community's future

    What you do

  • 1. Join your community

    Come together in a tight network of community members of all ages and backgrounds to learn about, discuss, and rally others around the local planning issues facing your community.

  • 2. Explore your issues

    Provide feedback to planners, debate the issues, and pose your own questions and calls for action over the course of a 3-6 week game. Each game is composed of a series of missions based on the main themes and concerns of the planning going on in your community.

  • 3. Map the future

    When you accomplish a mission, you earn the ability to unite with others and map the planning priories that you believe are most important for your community's future. All players will then be invited to a face-to-face meeting where they will come together with planners and decision-makers to discuss the results of the game.
